## How to Login

1. Open `loginpage.html` in your browser
2. Select your role (Student/Driver/Admin)
3. Enter your credentials
4. Click "Login"

## Security Notes

- Passwords are stored in plain text (for development only)
- In production, implement proper password hashing (bcrypt)
- Change default passwords after first login
- Implement password reset functionality
